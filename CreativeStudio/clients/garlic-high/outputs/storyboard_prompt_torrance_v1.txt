Brand concept:
Garlic High is a loud, irreverent, garlic-obsessed Japanese ramen shop with a Gen Z attitude. A new location opens in Torrance, California in 7 days. This ad is a countdown teaser warning the city that the chaos is coming — anime physics, impossible food energy, and a final brand reveal that says "TORRANCE — OPENING SOON."

Visual style:
90s anime illustrated style. Hand-drawn cel shading, painted backgrounds, warm sodium streetlight palette, late-night Tokyo-by-way-of-SoCal energy. Heavy ink linework, halftone shading in shadows, manga impact frames with radial speed lines and ground-crack hairlines, single bright flashes at moments of contact. Color palette anchored in deep reds, golden yellows, and forest greens lifted from the brand poster. Lighting is high-contrast, warm rim light, dramatic shadows. Camera feel: wide cinematic establishing shots, tight macro food shots, low angle hero frames, slight handheld energy on impact frames. Polish level: illustrated, not photoreal — painted ramen-shop ad with anime kinetic energy.

Video intent:
Each panel must feel like a plausible shot from a 15-second cinematic anime ad, not a random still image. The intended edit rhythm builds from quiet anticipation, into a sharp countdown text slam, into escalating chaos, peaking on an aura moment, and resolving into a confident brand reveal. Each frame should be liftable directly into a Seedance video generation as a sequential animated shot.

Storyboard format:
Generate one clean 10-panel storyboard contact sheet presented as a horizontal visual overview. Arrange the panels in a clear contact-sheet layout with consistent spacing and subtle visual separation. Do not include frame numbers, captions, arrows, callouts, written shot labels, or annotation marks of any kind. The sequence must read naturally from left to right, then top to bottom.

Panel aspect-ratio rule:
Every individual panel within the storyboard must itself be framed as a distinct 16:9 widescreen shot. This requirement applies to each scene, not just to the outer canvas. Each panel should clearly appear as a cinematic widescreen frame suitable for video. Do not generate square, near-square, or vertically ambiguous panels.

Story arc:
A quiet Torrance night cracks open as a chunky "7 DAYS" countdown text slams onto the sky. Anime chaos floods in — cleaver impact, raining garlic, bowl slam, food macro, aura burst from the mascot — then resolves into the Torrance shop materializing in the city and a final brand end card.

Frame-by-frame direction:
01 — Wide establishing shot of a quiet Torrance street at night. Palm tree silhouettes against a dark blue sky, warm sodium streetlight pooling on the pavement, distant neon glow rising on the horizon. Empty road. Calm before the storm. Low angle, deep depth.
02 — Bold chunky "7 DAYS" text in red, yellow, and green block letters slams across the frame from above, ink splatter framing the edges, radial speed lines radiating outward, halftone shading behind. Sky cracks open with light. Tight wide shot, text fills the frame.
03 — Low-angle close-up of a chef's cleaver crashing down onto a giant whole garlic head on a wooden cutting board. Anime impact frame with radial speed lines from the blade, ground-crack hairline forming beneath the board, single bright flash at the point of contact. Steam rises in the background.
04 — Mid-air slow-motion shot of garlic cloves and flakes raining down over the Torrance skyline. Palm trees and the silhouette of a city street below. Garlic suspended mid-fall in dramatic radial composition. Warm sodium streetlight cuts through the falling cloves. Medium wide shot, slight low angle.
05 — Tight medium shot of a ramen bowl crashing down on a wooden counter. Broth ripples outward in concentric circles, garlic pile on top shudders, single bright flash at contact, radial speed lines from the bowl. Steam jets sideways from the impact. Camera holds tight.
06 — Extreme macro close-up of the bowl from above. Glossy noodles, mountain of minced garlic, glistening egg yolk centered, thick golden column of steam rising vertically through the frame. Warm rim light from above. Hero food shot.
07 — Medium shot of the mascot, a young woman with brown hair and a pink terrycloth headband, in a black blazer, lifting chopsticks triumphantly. Slight smirk, single eye glint, neon rim light catching the edge of her face. Confident hero pose. Three-quarter angle.
08 — Wide shot from behind her — radial aura erupts behind her in red and gold, hair lifting from the energy, background blown out to deep red, anime power-up burst with radial light lines exploding outward. Single bright flash at the center.
09 — Wide establishing shot of the new Garlic High Torrance shop exterior at night. Japanese katakana neon sign blazing red and yellow above the door, warm interior light pouring out onto the wet pavement, a few shadowy silhouettes inside. The shop materializes confidently into the Torrance street from frame 01. Slight low angle.
10 — Final end-card-style frame. Bold chunky red, yellow, and green block typography reading "MORE GARLIC POR FAVOR" with "TORRANCE — OPENING SOON" stacked beneath. Manga ink-chaos borders, halftone shading, single garlic clove icon in the corner. Brand resolution. Tight composition, text fills the frame.

Continuity rules: Maintain the 90s anime illustrated style across every panel. Keep the brand color palette (red, yellow, green) running throughout. Keep mascot wardrobe consistent — black blazer, pink headband, brown hair.
