Create an 8-frame storyboard contact sheet for a short product advertisement, designed as a clean visual overview for video development.

Use the uploaded product images as the exact product references — one is a Jiro-style ramen bowl, one is a Mazesoba bowl. Preserve each bowl's proportions, toppings, broth color, noodle texture, and visual identity exactly in every frame where it appears. Do not recolor, redesign, or simplify either bowl.

Brand concept:
Garlic High serves Jiro-style ramen — a Tokyo-born bowl built on extra-thick noodles, rich all-day pork broth, and fresh garlic piled on with zero restraint. This ad introduces the concept to a Gen Z audience in bold fast-food commercial energy: bright yellow, food in motion, ingredient-forward, confident.

Visual style:
Primary background is flat saturated yellow — consistent across every panel, no gradients, no environments, no tables or surfaces visible. Product lighting is warm and appetite-forward: soft front key light, slight warm rim. Food is always the dominant element. Bold black 3D block typography appears ONLY in panels 01, 02, and 08 — nowhere else. Middle panels let the food carry the frame entirely.

Video intent:
Each panel suggests a distinct shot in a 15-second vertical commercial. The edit builds: brand title card → bowl reveal → food in motion → ingredient breakdown → Mazesoba introduction → brand close. Fast-food pacing — each frame is a distinct visual punch.

Storyboard format:
One clean 8-panel contact sheet arranged in 2 rows of 4 panels. Consistent spacing, subtle separation between panels. No frame numbers, captions, arrows, callouts, or annotation marks. Reads left to right, then top to bottom.

Panel aspect-ratio rule:
Every individual panel must be framed as a distinct 9:16 vertical shot. Tall, mobile-first. No horizontal, square, or landscape panels inside the grid.

Story arc:
Opens with the brand title card, reveals the bowl, shows food moving and alive, breaks down hero ingredients, introduces Mazesoba, closes on the brand. No characters, no hands — food only throughout.

Frame-by-frame direction:
01 — Bold yellow background. Centered title card: "WHAT IS JIRO-STYLE?" in massive black 3D block letters filling two-thirds of the vertical frame. "GARLIC HIGH · JIRO RAMEN" in bold black caps below. Chopped garlic pieces falling slowly in the background behind the text. No food bowl yet. Brand opener.
02 — Ramen bowl rising steadily from below, centered on yellow. Steam rising vertically from the broth surface. "JIRO RAMEN" in bold black 3D text anchored behind the bowl in the upper half. Product reveal.
03 — Chopsticks driving upward through dense coiled noodles. Thick glossy strands fanning outward mid-air. Broth droplets suspended. Motion energy — food in action. Yellow background. No text.
04 — Chopped garlic falling from the top of frame in slow motion. Individual cloves tumbling through yellow air. Garlic mountain visible on the bowl below. Pure food drama. Yellow background. No text.
05 — Overhead top-down of the full ramen bowl. All toppings spread clearly: chashu slices, bean sprouts, cabbage mound, garlic heap, pork back fat pools on the broth surface. Perfect symmetry. Bowl fills the vertical frame. Yellow background. No text.
06 — Close-up on thick pork chashu slices fanned across the bowl. Seared surface, fat edge glistening under warm key light. Faint steam drifting from the bowl edges. Appetite-forward. Yellow background. No text.
07 — Mazesoba bowl entering frame from the right, settling centered on yellow. Dry surface, layered toppings richly visible, no broth, no steam. Different texture entirely from the ramen. Yellow background. No text.
08 — Final brand frame: ramen bowl hero-centered on pure yellow. "GARLIC HIGH" in massive black 3D text dominates the upper third. "JIRO RAMEN" slightly smaller directly below it. Bowl centered underneath, steam rising from the broth surface. Clean brand close.

Composition rules:
Every panel is 9:16 vertical. Vary between: dynamic food action mid-motion (03, 04), tight ingredient close-ups (05, 06), reveal shots (02, 07), and brand frames (01, 08). 3D black text appears ONLY in panels 01, 02, and 08. Panels 03–07 are food only — no text. No human hands, arms, or faces in any panel.

Continuity rules:
Yellow background is the visual constant across all 8 panels. Warm front key lighting throughout. Ramen bowl maintains exact appearance in all ramen panels. Mazesoba bowl maintains exact appearance in its panels. Photoreal food photography quality throughout — not illustrated or stylized.

Negative constraints:
No frame numbers, captions, labels, or annotations. No hands, arms, or people. No dark or moody backgrounds — yellow flat field only. No horizontal or square panels inside the grid. No illustration — photoreal only. No blurry product. No garbled 3D text. No repetitive identical compositions. No 3D text in panels 03 through 07.
