Two best friends at their lockers on the first day. They wear the exact same skirt, identical in every detail, only the color is different. One girl wears a khaki pleated scooter skirt that hits just above the knee, with crisp pressed pleats across the front, two small decorative buttons on the front waistband, a clean stitched waistband, built in shorts underneath for full coverage, and a flat back panel with a plain elastic waistband and no pleats in the back. The other girl has full curly pigtails and wears the matching hunter green version of the very same skirt. Both girls wear the exact same white short sleeve collared polo shirt, identical tops. The camera moves slowly with them, close and alive, eye level, both girls fully in frame, their faces focused and real, mostly not smiling, concentrating on the locker and talking quietly. A bright upbeat female voice over kicks in: First day fits, and this pleated skirt is ready to go.

The khaki girl gives the door a firm pull and the top locker swings open, a quick real laugh of relief between them, the skirts shifting as they lean in, the pressed front pleats catching the gold light.

They both reach into the open top locker and grab a book, talking, one nudging the other, the busy hallway moving behind them. The upbeat voice over: Built-in shorts underneath, comfy and ready for anything.

The khaki girl pushes the top locker door shut and the two head off down the hall together toward class, books in their arms, the skirts swinging gently with their easy strides, the flat backs and plain elastic waistbands catching the warm gold light, their faces animated as they talk.
