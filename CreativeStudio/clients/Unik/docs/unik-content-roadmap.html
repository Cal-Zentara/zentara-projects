30 posts. Instagram and TikTok. June 1 to August 18. Every post has a purpose, a hook, and a clear goal. This is the exact content plan, week by week, — to build an audience of moms before back to school season starts.

Mom Research

We studied what moms actually save and share on Instagram and TikTok. The emotional content in Phase 1 comes from that research.

Content Strategy

Give value first, earn trust, then sell. Brands that skip straight to selling get ignored. That is the structure behind every phase.

The rule behind every post.

Nobody wakes up wanting to see a uniform ad. But every mom wakes up carrying weight that nobody acknowledges. The morning she had, the list she's managing, the invisible work she does every single day. UNIK's job is to see that. Show up there.. The product is secondary. The feeling comes first.

What success looks like

By August 18, UNIK should be the brand she already knows.

Saves over likes. A save means she wants to come back to it. That's the signal that content is working and she's ready to buy.

Comments from moms. When she tags another mom in the comments, UNIK has entered the community, not just the feed.

One post that breaks through. In 10 weeks, you'll have real data on what resonates. That's what informs next year and tells you when paid ads are worth testing.

Real UGC by week 10. If moms tag UNIK in their first day photos, the brand has crossed from content to community. That's the real win.
