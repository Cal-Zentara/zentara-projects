# UNIK Wild UGC — Locked Content Format
Created: June 5, 2026

## The Concept
A calm mom does a UGC-style product review of UNIK uniforms. Behind her something catastrophic is actively getting worse. She notices the danger but chooses the uniform anyway. Never shows the outcome. Cuts to end card at the peak.

---

## The Formula

| Element | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Character** | A calm mom. Talking directly to camera. Holding the product. |
| **Setting** | One wild environment — natural disaster, impossible location, or wild environment |
| **Urgency** | Builds progressively over 15 seconds. Gets worse the whole time. |
| **Her energy** | She notices. She chooses the uniform over it. Holds it up like it is the most important thing in the world. That is the joke. |
| **Proof line** | One price line before the cut. "Thirteen bucks." That is it. |
| **Ending** | Cut to end card at peak tension. Never show what happens. |

---

## The 15-Second Tension Arc

- **0 to 5s** — She is talking, background looks a little off but not alarming yet
- **5 to 10s** — Urgency builds, something is visibly getting worse behind her
- **10 to 14s** — It has peaked. She glances back, chooses the uniform, drops the price line
- **14 to 15s** — Hard cut to end card. Never show the outcome.

Tension builds the whole 15 seconds. Viewer cannot stop watching because it keeps getting worse.

---

## The 3 Setting Categories

**Natural Disasters** — urgency is environmental. The danger is visibly approaching her.
- Exploding volcano (lava creeping closer every second)
- Massive tidal wave building on the horizon
- Active lightning storm, bolts hitting the ground closer each time
- Meteor shadow growing on the ground around her

**Impossible Locations** — urgency is structural. Something in the location is failing.
- Submarine window cracking, water starting to drip
- Space station, hull breach alarm getting louder
- Plane going down, oxygen masks dropping one by one
- Shark tank glass cracking behind her

**Wild Environments** — urgency is alarm-based. Lights, sirens, things getting worse.
- Nuclear reactor meltdown, alarms escalating, hazmat workers sprinting past
- Building demolition, workers running, caution tape snapping
- Bomb on the wall, timer counting down
- Rocket launch pad, smoke building, ground shaking

---

## The Rules

1. Tension must BUILD. It is not just a wild background. It gets progressively worse over 15 seconds.
2. She notices. She does not dismiss it. She CHOOSES the uniform over it.
3. One proof line before the cut. Price only. "Thirteen bucks."
4. Never show the outcome. Cut at the peak.
5. Run ONE first. Measure conversion before committing to a series. Views without sales do not help UNIK.

---

## Why It Works
- Tension arc = viewers cannot stop watching (watch time)
- She chooses the uniform = product is the hero, not an afterthought
- Price line at peak = closes while attention is highest
- Never shows outcome = people comment asking what happened (engagement)
- Repeatable = new disaster every week, same format
