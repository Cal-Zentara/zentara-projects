## Quick reference

- **LOCKED marketing price claim (set June 3, 2026):** Brand level / blanket ads say **"under $20"** — honest across the whole core line (tops, bottoms, skirts, dresses, jumpers all fall under $20; only jackets and coats go above at $22.99+). Do NOT use "under $15" as a blanket claim — pants, shorts, skirts and jumpers all exceed $15, so it would mislead. For a **polo or top specific** ad you MAY use the stronger true number: **"polos under $15"** or **"polos from $12.99."**
- **Lead-with-price number:** most polos and blouses are **$12.99**.
- **Core uniform (polo + bottoms):** under ~$30 to dress a kid for the day.
- **Ranges:** tops $12.99–$14.99 · skirt/dress/jumper $14.99–$16.99 · shorts $14.99–$17.99 · pants $14.99–$18.99 · fleece $9.99–$15.99 · jackets & coats $22.99–$33.99.
- **Most common colors across the line:** white, navy, sky blue, burgundy, black, hunter green, khaki, grey, red.
- **Seasonal angle:** fleece + hoodies + puffers + sherpa coats = winter content after back-to-school.
