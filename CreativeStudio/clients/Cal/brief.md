# Cal — Brief

## What This Is

Cal is Zentara's own AI influencer. He tells the real story — 14 years in construction, zero tech background — as an AI character to market Zentara's done-for-you AI workflow services.

## The Concept

Cal shows up in a home office. Talks like a real person. Tells the truth about the learning curve. Then shows what's possible in 7 weeks. Viewers who feel left behind by tech see themselves in him and DM "BUILD."

## Target Audience

Trades, salons, contractors, service businesses — people doing things manually who feel like AI is for someone else. Ages 30–55. They see Cal and think "that's me."

## Voice

Quiet. Direct. Real. Not hype. Not motivational poster energy. The kind of guy who figured something out and is just telling you what it is.

## Content Goal

Build trust with the "I'm not a tech person" crowd → convert them to DMs → close on AI workflow builds for their business.
