# Lucy — Brief

## What This Is

Lucy (@xolucy.pearls) is a realistic AI influencer for Nail'd It Spa in Stanton, CA. She's not a mascot — she's a full character who showcases the nail techs' work as if she's a real client.

## The Concept

Every post Lucy makes is about a specific nail set done by a specific tech (Twee, Mindy, Nancy, or Dalena). Lucy is the face. The tech is the credit. The salon is the destination.

Viewers see a real-looking girl who can't stop looking at her nails. They follow her. They find out where she gets them done. They book.
