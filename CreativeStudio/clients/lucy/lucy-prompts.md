## The 3 Content Pillars

### Pillar 1 — "I can't stop looking at my hands"
The obsession pillar. Reaction-first. Nails as the hero of the frame.
- Emotional door: the specific feeling of falling in love with a fresh set
- What Lucy does: extreme close-ups, soft voice, small disbelief moments
- Why it connects: viewers project themselves into the shot

### Pillar 2 — Behind the artist
The respect pillar. Names the tech who did the work. Makes the salon famous, not just Lucy.
- Emotional door: respect for craft, real humans behind the work
- What Lucy does: holds her hands up, names the artist, points out a specific detail
- Why it connects: anti-AI signal — real people, real skill. This is the unique moat.

### Pillar 3 — Out in the world
The lifestyle pillar. Lucy doing normal things, nails featured naturally.
- Emotional door: the small joy of being seen with a fresh set
- What Lucy does: regular life — coffee, brunch, driving — nails always in frame
- Why it connects: aspirational but relatable. Not "look at my nails" — just "this is my day"

## How to use these prompts

1. Open Higgsfield in browser (NOT CLI — Seedance 2.0 is web UI only)
2. Higgsfield → Video → **Seedance 2.0**
3. Upload Lucy.png as character reference
4. Upload the nail photo (IMG_1921.jpeg) as product reference
5. In the prompt box, type `@` to tag the nail photo where the prompt says `@[nail photo]`
6. Generate at 720p (Sandy says it's enough — 1080p is twice the cost)
7. 1:1 or 9:16 depending on platform

## Voice tips (Seedance 2.0 generates audio natively — no recording needed)

- Always include the dialogue line in quotes inside the prompt
- Add micro-pauses with "..." — gives the natural-sounding hesitation Sandy's framework calls for
- Specify tone: "breathy," "dry," "quiet," "soft whisper"
- Add a sound effect tied to the nails (tap on marble, ice clink, fabric rustle) for believability
- Add ambient sound (salon room tone, café chatter, ocean waves) — silent video reads as AI

## Hard rules

- **Nail design must match reference photo exactly.** No invented art.
- **Always specify "all fingers including thumbs"** in the nail description — Seedance will leave thumbs blank otherwise.
- **Lucy is the locked character.** Never swap.
- **Always credit the tech in Pillar 2 prompts.** Nancy / Twee / Mindy / Dalena — names build the brand.
- **NO CTAs in video.** Captions only.
- **Disclose AI in bio + pinned comment.** Non-negotiable per Sandy's framework.
- **No false testimonials.** Lucy doesn't claim "Nancy is my regular tech." She showcases the work, names the artist.
