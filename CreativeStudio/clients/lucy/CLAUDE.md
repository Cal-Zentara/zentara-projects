## The 3 Content Pillars

### Pillar 1 — "I can't stop looking at my hands"
Obsession pillar. Reaction-first. Nails as hero.
- Extreme close-ups, soft voice, small disbelief moments
- Viewers project themselves into the shot

### Pillar 2 — Behind the artist
Respect pillar. Names the tech who did the work.
- Lucy holds her hands up, names the artist, calls out a specific detail
- Anti-AI signal — real people, real skill. The moat.

### Pillar 3 — Out in the world
Lifestyle pillar. Lucy doing normal things, nails in frame naturally.
- Coffee, brunch, driving — nails visible but not the focus
- Aspirational but relatable

## Hard Rules

- **Nail design must match the reference photo exactly.** Describe every finger: shape, base color, art, finish. Never invent.
- **Always specify "all fingers including thumbs"** — Seedance leaves thumbs blank otherwise.
- **Lucy is the locked character.** Never swap her. Always upload `assets/Lucy.png` to Higgsfield.
- **No CTAs in video.** Captions only.
- **Always credit the tech in Pillar 2 prompts.** Names build the brand.
- **Disclose AI in bio + pinned comment.** Non-negotiable.
- **No false testimonials.** Lucy showcases work, names the artist — she doesn't claim a personal relationship.
- **Hands stay off face/ear/cheek/chin.** Route hands to counter, hip, holding object, or spread toward camera.

## Generation Rules

- **Platform:** Higgsfield web UI — Seedance 2.0 lives there (NOT CLI)
- **Format:** 720p (Sandy's recommendation — 1080p is twice the cost for no visible gain at phone size)
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 or 9:16 depending on platform
- **Audio:** Seedance 2.0 generates dialogue natively — write the line in the prompt, it lip-syncs
- **Reference tags:** Use `@` to tag the nail photo inside Seedance — not just the character

### Voice tips (Seedance audio)
- Always include dialogue line in quotes inside the prompt
- Add micro-pauses with "..." for natural hesitation
- Specify tone: "breathy," "dry," "quiet," "soft whisper"
- Add a sound tied to nails (tap on marble, ice clink) for believability
- Add ambient sound (salon room tone, café chatter, ocean waves) — silent video reads as AI
