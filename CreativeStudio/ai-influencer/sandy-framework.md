## The Workflow (Step by Step)

1. Create AI influencer in Higgsfield (Character section) — or generate a character prompt in Claude → paste into Higgsfield → use Nano Banana Pro model, 1:1, 1K resolution
2. Download the influencer image (full body preferred)
3. Get the product or brand image
4. Run `/ai-influencer` — give it: influencer image + product image + brand URL
5. Claude outputs: 3 content pillars + Seedance 2.0 video prompts
6. Higgsfield → Video → Seedance 2.0 → paste prompt + upload influencer image + product image → generate at 720p
7. Publish — near-publish-ready, minimal editing needed

## Sandy's 12 Aspects

These are the 12 things baked into the `/ai-influencer` skill that make the content work:

1. **Parasocial psychology** — prompts that make viewers feel connected to the influencer
2. **Three content pillars** — keeps the account focused, not random
3. **Story-first content** — every video leads with a story, not a product pitch
4. **Consistent environment** — same couch, same room across all shots so the character feels real
5. **Audio quality** — natural room tone built into prompts to prevent the "AI feel"
6. **Four-part lighting** — avoids too-perfect AI skin that gives it away
7. **Reference image matching** — influencer stays in the same outfit across all content
8. **Emotional register matching** — the mood of the video matches the product/brand energy
9. **Brand voice learning** — give it a product URL and it learns how the brand talks
10. **Near-publish-ready output** — minimal editing needed after generation
11. **Free-form hooks** — attention-grabbing opening built into every prompt
12. **Multi-language support** — works for bilingual or international audiences

---

## Design Principles

- **Stand out visually** — Itana Lopez (pink hair + fitness) built 400K followers by owning a distinctive look. Pick a character that owns a lane.
- **Consistency is everything** — once you pick an influencer, use her for all content. Changing characters resets audience connection to zero.
- **Unique feature combos** — unusual combinations are memorable (red hair + blue eyes, etc.)
