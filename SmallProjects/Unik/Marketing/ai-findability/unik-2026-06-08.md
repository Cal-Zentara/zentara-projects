## Fix List

### This Week
- **Fix the two Yelp listings.** There are two UNIK Yelp pages with two different addresses. This tells AI and Google the business can't be trusted. Log into Yelp, claim the correct one (501 E. Pico), and request the other be removed or merged.
- **Ask 5 customers for a Google review this week.** Text or WhatsApp 5 regulars: "Hey, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps us. Here's the link: [link]." Five real reviews this week beats zero.
- **Make sure the Facebook page has the full address, phone, and hours.** Facebook is the #1 result when anyone searches the UNIK name — it should have everything a customer needs.

### This Month
- **Build up to 25 Google reviews.** AI assistants pull heavily from Google. At 25+ reviews with a 4.5+ rating, UNIK starts appearing in "best of" results. This is the highest-leverage thing they can do for AI findability.
- **Make sure the address and phone number are spelled exactly the same everywhere.** Google, Yelp, Facebook, YellowPages, and the website should all say: "501 E. Pico Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90015" and "(213) 748-3929." Even small differences hurt.
- **Respond to the 53 Birdeye reviews.** They have 4.8 stars there and have never responded. A few simple "thank you" replies signal to search engines the business is active.

### This Quarter
- **Get mentioned in a local LA blog or back-to-school guide.** One article in an LA parenting blog is worth more for AI findability than 10 directory listings. Reach out to 3-5 parent bloggers and offer a store visit or product sample.
- **Add a FAQ page to unikusa.com.** AI assistants pull answers from pages that directly answer questions. A page titled "School Uniform Size Guide — UNIK Los Angeles" gives AI something to cite.

AI assistants are becoming how parents find local businesses. When someone asks ChatGPT "where can I buy school uniforms in Los Angeles" — the businesses that show up get the click.
