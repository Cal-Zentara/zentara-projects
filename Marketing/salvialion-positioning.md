# Positioning — Salvia Lion
Last updated: April 27, 2026

## One-Line Statement
I help people who absorb the energy of every room they walk into reset, cleanse, and feel like themselves again — through handmade bath salts charged with healing intentions by a practicing Reiki healer.

## Who It's For
Empaths and sensitive people who leave work, crowds, or difficult situations feeling drained and heavy. They're either just starting their spiritual journey — excited but unsure — or they already know they absorb others' energy and need a simple ritual to clear it at home.

## What Makes It Different
Every bag is made and charged by Salvia during her Reiki practice. She's not a maker who added "intentions" as a marketing phrase — she's a Reiki healer who experienced energy absorption herself, found healing through it, and now makes these for people like her.

## Proof Points
- Salvia's own story: Reiki ended her chronic headaches and anxiety attacks when nothing else worked
- Clients leave sessions saying they feel "relaxed or sleepy" — a real physical response, not a promise
- "If I only reach 1 person, that's enough" — she's not chasing scale, she's chasing impact
