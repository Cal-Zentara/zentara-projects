## Who Buys (Buyer Research — Reddit + Etsy, 2026-04-27)

**Three buyer types:**
1. **Empaths / sensitive people** — "I absorb others' energy." Buying relief from feeling drained after work, crowds, difficult people.
2. **Spiritual beginners** — Just starting their journey, excited but unsure. Want something physical they can do at home without needing special skills.
3. **Stressed professionals** — Use it after hard weeks, crowded places, intense situations. The ritual is the appeal.

**Why they buy (triggers):**
- Scent is #1 — always lead with it in every post and listing
- Instructions included — "how to use" ritual copy is a proven conversion driver on Etsy
- Maker's story — handmade by a Reiki healer = more powerful than factory-made
- Specific outcomes — not "relax" but "clear, grounded, protected"
- Packaging + presentation signal legitimacy

**Exact buyer language to use in all copy:**
- "I absorb others' energy"
- "After a long week at work, crowded places"
- "Charged with intentions"
- "Feel grounded, calm, protected"
- "Reset / cleanse / clear my energy"
- "People who feel everything"
- "Energetic shield"
- "Aura cleansing"

**Nancy's biggest differentiator:**
Nobody on Etsy positions as a practitioner who makes the salts as part of their healing practice. Salvia is a Reiki healer who charges every bag. That's the gap nobody else fills. Every piece of copy should say this.

## Etsy as a Sales Channel

Worth opening an Etsy shop once Nancy has:
- 5–10 reviews (social proof minimum)
- Professional product photos
- Stripe live (so she has fulfillment workflow in place)

**Etsy tradeoff:**
- Pro: 90M+ buyers already searching, built-in trust, free discovery
- Con: 6.5% transaction fee + listing fees, algorithm-dependent

**Strategy:** Use IG + website to build brand and collect reviews. Open Etsy as a second channel to capture search traffic. Cross-link everything.
