## Before You Post — The Foundation

Three questions every creator must answer first:
- **Message** — what belief drives your brand?
- **Avatar** — who exactly are you talking to?
- **Uniqueness** — what's your story, skills, and perspective that nobody can copy?

Most creators skip this and wonder why nothing sticks.

---

## The 3-1-2-1 Weekly System

7 posts a week, each with a specific job:

| Posts | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 3x | Attract | Hook strangers with relatable pain or story |
| 1x | Nurture | Behind-the-scenes, make them trust you |
| 2x | Position | Share insights that build authority |
| 1x | Convert | One clear ask — buy, book, DM, join |

Most creators only attract and never convert. That's why they have followers but no money.

---

## How to Hook Someone in 3 Seconds

Script formula: **Problem → Promise → Credibility → Delivery → CTA**

- First frame must trigger an emotion: curiosity, surprise, or concern
- Study 10–15 creators you respect, pull their top posts, ask: *why did this stop the scroll?* Rebuild it with your own angle.
- If click-through is high but retention is low, your hook is making a promise the video doesn't deliver.

## The Silent Film Reel

No talking. Just music + B-roll + text on screen.
His highest-performing top-of-funnel format.

**Why it works:**
- Works with sound off (most people scroll muted)
- Forces clarity — if the text isn't sharp, the video fails
- Outperforms talking-head for getting new eyes

## Your Story Is Your Niche

Traditional niche advice ("be the fitness guy") is wrong. Make *yourself* the niche.
- Weave your story into everything — background, age, struggles, perspective
- People follow *you*, not a topic
- Can't be copied
- He grew 8M as "Jun the person" — fitness + business + self-care + family, all one feed

The three pillars of uniqueness:
1. **Story** — your background, challenges, where you came from
2. **Skills** — what you're capable of
3. **Perspective** — how you see things differently

---

## The Batching System

- Film in 10–30 second clips, not long takes
- Get 3 angles per shot (wide, side, zoomed) — triples usable footage from one session
- 50+ labeled folders in Dropbox — pre-shot B-roll ready to grab anytime
- One batch day = weeks of content library
- **3-take rule:** 3 tries per shot, then move on. Perfectionism kills momentum.

## Monetization Path

Free content → low ticket → mid ticket → high ticket

1. Free content builds trust
2. Digital products (~$99) — first revenue
3. Courses / subscription — recurring revenue
4. Coaching / done-for-you — high ticket

The **Convert post (1x/week)** is what moves people through this funnel. Without it, you're growing an audience for someone else to sell to.

---

## The 100 Video Rule

Commit to 100 videos before judging if it's working.
Most people quit after 10. The first 100 are data collection, not performance.
