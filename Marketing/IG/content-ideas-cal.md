# Cal — IG Content Ideas
*Built: 2026-04-29 | Framework: Jun Yuh 3-1-2-1*

---

## Attract (3x/week)
Hook strangers with a relatable story or moment.

- "14 years in construction. Zero tech background. First build the same week I picked up Claude."
- "I told Claude what I wanted — a fitness app that tracks workouts, meals, and macros. I had no idea what I was doing. It worked."
- "She dropped 3 files on her computer. They showed up on her Instagram as a carousel. She didn't touch anything else."
- "One approval now posts to Instagram and Facebook at the same time. She doesn't know any of this is happening."
- "She had 6 phone videos and no idea what to do with them. We turned it into a Reel in one session."
- "Built a full website, bought the domain, and set up Google Business for a client in one day. Start to finish."
- "I was about to charge a client $150 for something. Then I found out her booking app already did it for free. Here's what I did instead."
- "One wrong assumption cost me 3 hours. Here's what it was."

---

## Position (2x/week)
Teach something. Build trust.

- Why you never say "AI" when talking to someone who doesn't use it
- Why free work was worth it — and when to stop
- The difference between doing it for someone vs teaching them how
- Why coming from a completely different field makes you better, not worse
- How to know when to charge for something vs give it away
- Checking if the tool already exists before you build it
- The moment you realized you could actually do this
- Why simple beats impressive every time
- What it feels like to show someone something and watch it click for them

---

## Nurture (1x/week)
Human side. Make people like you, not just respect you.

- The day you quit construction and had no idea what was next
- What it actually looked like the first week — no background, just figuring it out
- A client reaction that surprised you
- Building something that didn't work — then it did
- Why you started this — the real answer, not the polished one
- What 14 years of construction taught you that nobody in tech has

---

## Convert (1x/week)
One clear ask. No fluff.

- "I have one Build spot open this week. DM me."
- "Want me to set this up for your business? DM 'BUILD'."
- "I do a free 20-minute call. You leave knowing exactly what's possible. Link in bio."
- "This is what the $300 build looks like. Want one? DM me."
