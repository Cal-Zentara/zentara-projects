# Daily Playbook

Open this before you post. Read it. Close it.

---

## Step 1 — Pick Your Post Type Today

Rotate through this order each week:

| Day | Type | Job |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Attract | Hook a stranger with a real story |
| Tue | Position | Teach something, build trust |
| Wed | Attract | Another story or moment |
| Thu | Convert | One clear ask — DM BUILD |
| Fri | Position | Share an insight or lesson |
| Sat | Attract | Story or client result |
| Sun | Nurture | Human side — make people like you |

---

## Step 2 — Pick a Hook

**Attract hooks (stop the scroll with a real moment):**
- "14 years in construction. Zero tech background. First build the same week I picked up Claude."
- "She dropped 3 files on her computer. They showed up on her Instagram as a carousel. She didn't touch anything else."
- "She had 6 phone videos and no idea what to do with them. We turned it into a Reel in one session."
- "Built a full website, bought the domain, and set up Google Business for a client in one day. Start to finish."
- "I was about to charge a client $150 for something. Then I found out her booking app already did it for free."
- "One wrong assumption cost me 3 hours. Here's what it was."

**Position hooks (teach something):**
- Why you never say "AI" when talking to someone who doesn't use it
- The difference between doing it for someone vs teaching them how
- Why simple beats impressive every time
- Checking if the tool already exists before you build it
- Why coming from a completely different field makes you better, not worse
- Why the business owner who thinks tech isn't for them is exactly who this is built for
- What "feeling left behind by tech" actually costs a business every week

**Nurture hooks (human side):**
- The day you quit construction and had no idea what was next
- What it actually looked like the first week — no background, just figuring it out
- A client reaction that surprised you
- What 14 years of construction taught you that nobody in tech has

**Convert (one clear ask):**
- "I have one Build spot open this week. DM me."
- "Want me to set this up for your business? DM BUILD."
- "This is what a done-for-you build looks like. Want one? DM me."

> **SCRIPTS READY TO USE → open Content Ideas in the KB.**

---

## Step 3 — Voice Check (30 seconds)

**Do:**
- Reaction first, then the lesson — *"She went quiet. Then: wait, it can do that?"*
- Specific. "Built in 8 days" not "built fast." "Nail salon in Stanton" not "a client."
- Short sentences. One thought per line.

**Don't:**
- Hype words — game-changer, unlock, leverage, seamless, incredible, amazing
- Exclamation marks
- Say "AI" — say Claude, systems, tools

**Every post ends with:**
> "DM me 'BUILD' if you want this for your business."

---

## Step 4 — Format

| Platform | Format |
|---|---|
| Instagram carousel | Hook slide → 3 story slides → CTA slide. Caption under 100 words. No hashtags. |
| TikTok / YouTube Shorts | Face to camera. 60–90 seconds. Reaction first, then the lesson, then "DM me BUILD." |
| Facebook | Same voice, slightly more personal. Proof-heavy. |

---

## When Someone DMs You

Don't freeze. Four steps:

1. Reply within 2 hours: *"Hey — quick question: do you want to learn how to build this yourself, or do you just want it handled for you?"*
2. **"Learn it"** → Teach offer ($500/session). Book a call.
3. **"Handled"** → Ask 3 questions: what do you do, what's the repetitive task, how much time does it eat per week.
4. *"I can put together a plan and quote. Want me to?"*

---

## When It Feels Pointless

Weeks 3–5 feel like nothing is working. Views flat. Crickets. That's the invisible phase, not the slow phase. Most people quit here. That's your advantage.

36 posts in teaches you more than 2 years of planning.

**Rule #6:** The work you avoid is the work that pays.
**Rule #7:** You're not in the slow phase. You're in the invisible phase.
**Rule #8:** 14 years of construction. Nobody else in this space has that story. Lead with it every time.
