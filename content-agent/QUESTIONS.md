# Content Agent — By-Hand Question System

Run this on any account. Three steps, always in this order.

1. Read the account (what it sells, who it is for)
2. Find the customer words (their real language, why they come back)
3. Ask Meta AI the 6 trend questions

Customer words first. Trends second. Then go post.

---

## STEP 1 — Customer words (ask yourself, or paste reviews to Claude)

```
What do my customers actually say about why they come back? What exact words do they use? What problem did they tell me I solved?
```

Best hooks come from here. This part does not need Meta AI.

---

## STEP 2 — Ask Meta AI these 6, in order

Fill in [my niche] and [my customer] for the account you are on.

```
For [my niche] on Instagram and Facebook right now, what content formats and hooks are getting the most engagement? Give me real examples with dates. Name your source. If you cannot verify something is working right now, say so instead of guessing.
```

```
Out of those, rank them best to worst by what is actually performing this month. For each one tell me how you know it is working and name the source.
```

```
Forget the product label. My customer is [my customer]. What makes them stop scrolling, trust a brand, and actually buy? Use real examples with dates and name sources.
```

```
Find 3 to 5 accounts in my niche winning right now. For each one break down their hooks, their formats, how often they sell versus teach, and what their top posts have in common.
```

```
What audio and what hooks are trending in my niche this week that I can put my own spin on? Tell me where each one comes from. Be honest if you cannot confirm it is currently trending.
```

```
Now make it dead simple. Give me the 3 things to post this week, in plain English, with the exact hook line for each.
```

---

## The one rule

Every Meta question ends with "name your source, be honest if you cannot verify."
Treat Meta trends as leads to check, not facts. Your customer words are the truth.
