# Outbound Lead Generation Methods

A reference for building outbound systems for any client. Same pipeline every time — find people, get their email, send a personalized message. Only the source changes based on the industry.

## The 9 Methods

1. **AirScale + AnyMailFinder** — search for companies by type, get their domains, tool guesses the email addresses
2. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Vain** — find decision makers on LinkedIn, export them, enrich with emails
3. **Enterprise domain dump** — put a big company's domain into AnyMailFinder and get every public email at that company at once (cheap — 1 credit = 40 emails)
4. **Apollo scraper** — similar to LinkedIn but through Apollo's database (getting shaky as of early 2025)
5. **Google search scraper** — scrape Google results for "[niche] + [location]" searches, get their websites, find emails
6. **Google Maps scraper** — same idea but for local businesses on Maps (~20-30% enrichment rate)
7. **Social media scraping** — scrape Instagram/TikTok/X profiles, pull emails from bios and links (~20-40% enrichment)
8. **LinkedIn Jobs** — find companies actively hiring for a specific role = they have money and a real need right now
9. **Custom scraping** — build your own scraper for niche platforms nobody else is targeting (can hit 11%+ reply rates)


## The One Thing That Changes Per Industry

Where you find the leads. The pipeline is always the same:
- DTC brands → Google search / AirScale
- Local businesses → Google Maps
- Enterprise → LinkedIn Sales Nav / domain dump
- Loan officer leads → Realtors, Zillow, LinkedIn
- Hiring targets → LinkedIn Jobs

## Tools Referenced

- **AirScale** — company search by type/keyword
- **AnyMailFinder** — email enrichment from domain or person name
- **Vain** — LinkedIn scraper (needs LI_AT token)
- **Apollo** — contact database with built-in emails
- **Apify** — hosts scrapers for Google, Maps, Instagram, LinkedIn Jobs, etc.
- **Instantly** — cold email platform with built-in lead database (250-300M leads, $97/mo for 4,500)
- **Bright Data** — bulk list purchase marketplace (cheaper but older data, ~half the performance)
