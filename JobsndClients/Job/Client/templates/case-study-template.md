# Case study template

Your #1 selling tool. A case study is proof you've done it before. Format is always the same three beats: **Problem → What I built → Result.** Keep it short and concrete; real numbers beat adjectives.

---

## [Client / Project Name]

**Service:** Web / Automation / Social
**Date:** [month, year]

### The problem
What was broken or costing them. One or two sentences. Example: "Joe's Coffee had no mobile site and was losing walk-ins who couldn't find their hours or location on a phone."

### What I built
Plain description of what you delivered. Example: "A fast, one-page mobile-first site with hours, menu, directions, and a click-to-call button."

### The result
The payoff, with a number if you have one. Example: "Mobile visits up 40%, and three new catering inquiries in the first month." If you don't have a number yet, use a concrete observation: "Owner stopped getting 'are you open?' calls."

### Proof (optional)
- Before/after screenshot
- A quote from the client
- Link to the live work
