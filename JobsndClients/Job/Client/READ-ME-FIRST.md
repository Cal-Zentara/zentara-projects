## The 7 stages, in one breath

**Find** a lead → **Qualify** if they're worth it → **Prep** your proposal/portfolio → **Reach out** personally → **Prep for the call** → **Close** the deal → **Onboard and follow up.**

That's your whole pipeline. The CLAUDE.md tells me exactly what to do at each step so you don't have to.

## The "tracker" — your project's memory

Chat history disappears. Files don't. So I'll keep your pipeline in three plain files in this folder:

- **leads.md** — every potential client and what to do next.
- **clients.md** — people you're working with or have worked with.
- **templates/** — your reusable proposals and messages, so nothing starts from scratch.

When a lead moves forward, I update the file, not just the chat. That way you can always open the folder and see where everything stands.

## What I think of those 6 GitHub repos (the honest version)

You sent me six. **All six are built for job seekers** — people sending a resume to an employer. You're selling services to clients, which is a different game. So most of them don't fit. Here's the straight talk:

| Repo | My verdict |
|---|---|
| **career-ops** | **Worth copying the structure.** It's the only one built like a proper Claude project — a tracker plus modular "modes." I borrowed its skeleton for your CLAUDE.md and threw away its resume content. |
| **ResumeSkills** | **Worth raiding for parts.** It's 20 little Claude "skills." About 5 can be rewritten for you: cover letter → proposal, portfolio case study → sales asset, interview prep → call prep, salary negotiation → pricing, job analyzer → lead qualifier. The other 15 are resume stuff you don't need. |
| **ApplyPilot** | **Skip it — and avoid the thinking behind it.** It auto-blasts 1,000 job applications. For client work, mass-spamming is the fastest way to wreck your reputation and get blocked. Clients come from real, personal outreach. |
| **reactive-resume** | **Skip.** A resume-building app. You don't need a resume, and it's heavy to run. |
| **Resume-Matcher** | **Skip.** It matches a resume to a job posting. There's no job posting in your world. |
| **Smart-AI-Resume-Analyzer** | **Skip.** Resume scorer, and a bit sloppily built (it ships with a default password in the open). |

**Bottom line:** none of the six are plug-and-play for you, because none were made for selling services. The good ideas — a tracker, modular skills, personalized (not mass) outreach — are already baked into your CLAUDE.md.
